How to submit an egg discovery:

You can send an email to triplefaultgames@gmail.com, send it to us on twitter, fill out this submission form, or send a message in the game's discord server, where we will have a channel dedicated to discussing the easter egg hunt.

When submitting a discovery, include a video and/or screenshot of you finding the egg, and please mention how you'd like to be credited on this page, if at all.

Good luck, and happy hunting!
