Sir Happenlance Egg Hunt

Following is a list of all 30 eggs, with information about the ones that have been discovered so far. The list will be continually updated as more eggs are found. If you've found an egg that isn't in the list yet, let me know by one of the methods listed at the bottom of this page.

How to submit an egg discovery:

You can send an email to triplefaultgames@gmail.com, send it to us on twitter, fill out this submission form, or send a message in the game's discord server, where we have a channel dedicated to discussing the easter egg hunt.

When submitting a discovery, include a video and/or screenshot of you finding the egg, and please mention how you'd like to be credited on this page, if at all.

Good luck, and happy hunting!
